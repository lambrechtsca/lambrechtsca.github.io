Concerts

The Don't Look Down Tour

9/23/23 -- The Sylvee

Raffaella

Raffaella was a really fun and energizing opener. I had never heard her songs before, and I definitely left wanting to check her out more!

Misterwives

Misterwives was, by far, the main reason I wanted to go to this concert, and they did not disappoint! They had the highest energy, most dance-able set I've probably ever been to.

Bishop Briggs

Bishop Briggs did a great job closing out this show with her powerful vocals and empowering songs.

Let the Bad Times Roll Tour

8/29/23 -- American Family Insurance Ampitheater

Simple Plan

Simple Plan's set was a blast from the past the whole way through. I especially loved their performance of What's New Scooby Doo with a man in a Scooby Doo costume running around stage shooting tshirts into the crowd.

Sum41

Sum41 has been one of my favorite bands since childhood, and finally seeing them in concert was a very special experience. They had great energy and sounded even better in person than I could've ever imagined!

The Offspring

With super fun and crazy visuals aided by funny bits in between songs, The Offspring put on a great show. I had a blast closing out such a great night dancing along to some of their most iconic songs.

The Wrecks: Back and Better Than Ever Tour

10/19/22 -- Majestic Theatre

Carr

Carr appealed perfectly to the audience filled with mostly college-aged women, singing and joking around about relatable dating mishaps and connecting with the crowd. Super fun set!

The Wrecks

The last time I saw The Wrecks in concert was 5 years before this one, and how much they have improved since then absolutely blows my mind. The vocals, the instruments, the energy overall--all were just incredible. I can't wait to see them again!
