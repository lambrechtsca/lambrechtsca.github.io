Favorites

Current Favorites

Songs

Blindfold by Bombay Bicycle Club (feat. Liz Lawrence)
I was not in love with this song at first listen, but then I suddenly couldn't stop coming back to it. Liz's unique voice defines this relaxing tune.

Fast Car by Tracy Chapman
I have not been able to stop thinking about this song since Chapman's recent Grammy's performance--so powerful and emotional.

Your Apartment by Wallows
Wallows' newest release and it is a lot of fun!

Albums

The Liftaway by Walk the Moon
Walk the Moon's final release before taking an indefinite hiatus. A very bittersweet release for long-time fans, but overall, it is worth a listen.

Dripfield by Goose
My favorite album to listen to while studying because it is simultaneously relaxing and exciting enough to keep me focused.

Immunity by Clairo
With an overall mood similar to rainy days with a hint of sunshine, this is a great album for the springtime.

Artists

Last Dinosaurs
With their two new high-energy singles, N.P.D. and Keys to Your Civic, Last Dinosaurs is the perfect band to check out as the weather warms up for spring.

Bombay Bicycle Club
With a discography chock full of amazing albums, their new EP Fantasies is a new, more relaxing take on their sound. I recommend just about everything they've ever made.

Misterwives
An incredibly underrated band that has released countless high-energy, feel-good songs over the last decade.
