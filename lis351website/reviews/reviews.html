Nobody Loves Like You by Atlas Genius

Atlas Genius is a band I really like, and it was exciting to see them release some new singles this year! This one was definitely my favorite due to the catchy chorus and overall good vibes, but it can be a bit repetitive at times and doesn't stand up super well against their past songs. I still like and plan to listen to it more, though!

Rating: 3.5/5

Stay by Post Malone

An incredibly relaxing song that is one of my favorites to listen to on rainy days. Whether you are studying, on a walk, driving, or just relaxing at home, this is one of those songs that always feels nice to listen to.

Rating: 4.5/5

Have U Seen Her by Kid Bloom

This song (and Kid Bloom in general) is perfect for anyone looking for something upbeat to listen to. With its fun sound and dramatic lyrics, this song feels like it has something for everyone that likes energetic indie pop.

Rating: 4/5

Soulja Boy Tellem by Soulja Boy

This is one of my favorite songs ever, but I have flawed taste. If you also like some songs just because they're silly, this is a great song for you!

Rating: 2/5
